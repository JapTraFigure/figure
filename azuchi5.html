Doyusha's 1/550 scale Azuchi Castle [5/6]

I also apply a dab of gold to the tips of the tiles. I decided to finish the roof tiles of the castle tower of Azuchi Castle in gray. Now, let's move on to the interesting task of castle modeling: the expression of the lawn.

This is the stone wall of the base.

Here it is! The green powder that came with the kit!　If you've missed it, you've probably made it before.

These are powders that came with another kit I bought in the past.　All are slightly different colors... How do you work with it?

Green powder is held in place by paint, not glue. The key is to choose a green paint, darker than the color of the powder.

Apply the paint in the bottle to the sides of the stone wall with a brush.　Masking is not necessary. Just a little bit on the stone wall is good.

Next, before the paint dries, sprinkle the green powder on the surface with a pinch of your finger. Your daily cooking skills will be put to the test (^_^;)

Sprinkle generously. Be sure to do this inside the kit box.

Then, lift up the base and pop the corners with your fingers, and the excess green powder will fall down and collect in the box. Without the box, the desk would be covered in powder, and the whole room would be dusty (；´∀｀)
The powder that accumulates in the box is used to sprinkle again.

Repeating this process completes the expression of the lawn.

Completed images will be next time.
