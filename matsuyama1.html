Doyusha's 1/450 scale Matsuyama Castle [1/6]

It is the proud symbol of Matsuyama City, the capital of Ehime Prefecture. Also known as Kinkijo Castle, Katsuyama Castle. It is one of the 12 existing castles in Japan. It is released by Doyusha, a model manufacturer.

Ehime Prefecture and Matsuyama City are also a land full of historical romance, such as Bo-chan, Clouds over the Hill, and Dogo Hot Springs.




Now, let's open the package!

This contents! I miss it. I remember making it once in my childhood. I remember sprinkling some green powder on it to recreate a lawn...I miss it so much.

A brightly colored catalog is included.

There are models of armor and swords. They are glittering. It is hard to believe that they are plastic models.

Now, it's time to start production. The tension is so high that I can't stand it any longer. I temporarily assemble the parts immediately. This is it! This is the atmosphere!

These are parts of roof tiles.

The keep is already taking shape. I think the key to make it well is to process the burrs of the parts neatly and to paste the walls together on the foundation. The key to making it well, I think, is to clean up the burrs on the parts and to paste the walls together on the base.




Oh, it's so fun!




What an exciting feeling, as if I had entered the world of Hana no Keiji or NHK Taiga.




When it is completed, I will be the owner of a castle in my own country! Ho-ho-ho.
