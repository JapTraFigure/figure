Masking tape was applied to cover the stone wall.

This is what it looks like in close-up. Unlike the world where even a 0.1mm mistake is not allowed when making a plastic model of a car, making a model of a castle is relatively easy because it can be done roughly.

Finally, it is time to paint the ground.Paint the ground with a fluffy coat of dark yellow. No solid paint.

Then paint with tan. This is also sprayed fluffy.

Fun masking tape peeling.

The "first stage" of painting of stone walls and ground was completed.

The corner of the stone wall is failing. It needs to be corrected. Painting the keep. Painting the keep of Nobunaga Oda, the High King, is a fun part of the project.

The painting of Azuchi Castle is a matter of personal preference, as there are only a few surviving documents of the castle. The Azuchi Castle that I will build will be a symbol of the new age of military rule under the heaven. I aim to create a "gorgeous and gorgeous" castle. The top floor of the castle tower will be painted in gold, as if it were covered with gold leaf.

The parapet of the keep will also be painted gold.

The nameplate of Azuchi Castle will also be painted gold.



The roof tiles will be painted.

The tiles will be painted in neutral gray.

The eave area will be painted white.

Mark the roof tile area. Apply a coat of thinned flat black.

Wipe with solvent to complete inking.

The lower part of the keep should be painted with Super Italian Red 2 because we want it to be crimson.

Remove the masking.

It is going to be a flashy keep. The interior is also painted in gold to resemble a gold leaf covering.

The orca is also painted gold and temporarily assembled. I will look at it carefully and confirm the image. I would like to paint more details with a brush in the future.
