Doyusha's 1/450 scale Matsuyama Castle [4/6]

This time we are painting the white wall.

Fix the parts to disposable chopsticks with double-sided tape bought at a 100 yen store.

Paint the white wall with Gundam color MS White.

Paint the tiles neutral gray.

I'll do some inking and gradation painting later.

This is the size of the keep. Surprisingly small, right?

It is about 4 cm.
Compared to a 1 yen coin, the turret is quite small.

After painting the white walls, the next step is to prepare the planks for painting.
Masking tape cut into 2 to 3 mm squares is also used.

Reading glasses are indispensable.




I want to bring out the texture of the wooden structure.
